A weak purpose is a primary source of prompt failure, leading to irrelevant outputs, wasted resources, and difficult evaluation. A clear purpose is the bedrock of an effective prompt. * **Pitfall:** Overly broad purpose (e.g., "Improve text," "Write about topic X").
